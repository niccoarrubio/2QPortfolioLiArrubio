VALORANT

An Overview Of: The Initiator

Initiators are one of the four roles that agents are divided into in VALORANT.

The abilities of agents under this role focus on leading attacks, gathering information, and creating openings for their team to enter contested areas.

They use abilities like flashes, recon tools, and concussive effects to disrupt enemies, reveal their positions, and clear angles on both attack and defense. The goal is to support their team by safely challenging enemy positions and enabling a successful push onto a site.

As of October 2025, the game currently has seven Initiator agents in its roster.
